House of Panchhi

Panchhi is an Indian fashion brand that specializes in ethnic clothing for women. The brand aims to provide supreme quality and excellent craftsmanship to its customers. They also emphasize the importance of preserving India's rich heritage by embracing traditional structures and combining them with different colors, textures, and silhouettes.

The House of Panchhi team is dedicated to experimenting with traditional designs while staying true to their roots. They constantly work on innovating India's traditional ethnic wear to match the current fashion trends, while upholding the inherent elegance of such clothing. Panchhi takes pride in serving true Indian tradition that adds happiness to every occasion while keeping the prices budget-friendly
